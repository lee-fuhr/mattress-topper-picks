How we choose our picks

We base our recommendations on long-term owner feedback collected from thousands of verified purchasers, as well as in-depth reviews from trusted sleep experts and publications. We prioritise toppers with a strong track record for maintaining their original shape after six months or more of nightly use, and we look closely at what breaks or disappoints over time, whether that’s permanent indentations, cover seams splitting, or annoying off-gassing that doesn’t dissipate.
