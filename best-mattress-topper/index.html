A mattress topper is one of the most cost-effective ways to change the feel of your bed without buying a whole new mattress. Whether your current bed is too firm, too soft, too warm, or just starting to show its age, the right topper can add years of comfort and support. The key is matching the material, memory foam, latex, or down alternative, to your sleeping style and body type. When choosing a topper, focus on three things: thickness (from an inch to four inches), density or firmness, and temperature regulation. Memory foam toppers excel at contouring and pressure relief but can trap heat; latex toppers are bouncier, more breathable, and usually last longer. Down-alternative options are often cheaper and washable but provide less support.
