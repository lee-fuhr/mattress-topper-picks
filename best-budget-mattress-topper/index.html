Best budget mattress toppers

Quick answer

The Lucid 3-inch Gel Memory Foam Topper is our top budget pick for its balance of pressure relief and cooling at a very affordable price.

The Sleep Innovations 4-inch Dual Layer suits those who want a plusher feel on a modest budget, while the Milliard 2-inch is ideal for thin toppers that won't change mattress height much.

A mattress topper is one of the cheapest ways to change the feel of your bed without buying a whole new mattress. It can soften a too-firm surface, add pressure relief for aching joints, or revive a sagging lump. But budget toppers are a crowded market: many start around a modest outlay, but not all hold up. The best ones use a good foam density, a reasonable thickness (most are 2 to 4 inches), and some cooling feature. Our researched picks below focus on models that have strong owner feedback and positive reviews from trusted experts, all without breaking the bank. When shopping for a budget topper, focus on three things: the foam type (memory foam or polyfoam), thickness, and a removable cover. Gel-infused memory foam is common at low prices and helps with heat. Thinner toppers (2 inches) are easier to install and suit those who just want a slight tweak; thicker ones (3 or 4 inches) provide deeper body contouring. A washable cover keeps the topper fresh. We selected three well-known products that cover different needs: the best all-round value, a plusher dual-layer option, and a compact low-profile pick.
